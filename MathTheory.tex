\subsection{Pythagorean Triples}
The Pythagorean triples are uniquely generated by $a=k(m^2-n^2)$, $b=k(2mn)$, $c=k(m^2+n^2)$ where $m>n>0, k>0, gcd(m,n)=1$, and either $m$ or $n$ is even.
